About Me

My introduction

As a Certified Project Management Professional and UPenn graduate with over 7 years of experience, Scarlett Luis has honed her skills in improving products and processes. After starting her career as an engineer, she transitioned to becoming a Product Owner, thanks to her strong technical and business acumen.

Scarlett's passion for education and empowering underrepresented groups has led her to coach college students and recent graduates in career development, job search strategies, and interview preparation. She's on a mission to bridge the gap in access to education, resources, and opportunities for first-generation college graduates, first-generation professionals, and students from low-income families. This comes from her own experience as a first-generation college graduate, first-generation professional, and the first engineer in her family.

When she's not improving products or processes, Scarlett can be found coaching and speaking to aspiring STEM professionals or volunteering for causes that support economic empowerment. She believes in helping as many people as possible navigate their unknowns when pursuing a higher-education degree or career.
